# Game Manual: Complete Player Guide

## Part 1: Starting Your Journey

### Main Menu

When you first open the game, you see the main menu with the **five emotions** (Joy, Sadness, Anger, Disgust, Fear) displayed at the bottom.

**Menu Buttons:**

- **Start Game**: Press this to enter the mode selection screen.
- **Settings**: Adjust game audio, visual preferences, and other options here.
- **Exit**: Close the game.

---

## Part 2: Mode Selection Screen

After pressing **Start Game**, you'll see three game modes:

### Available Modes

- **STORY** (Yellow text, Available from the start)  
  The main story campaign. Start here to unlock other modes.

- **EMOTION MATCHING** (Light gray text with a lock icon, Initially locked)  
  A matching game where you tap the correct emotions based on situations.  
  **Unlocks when you:** Complete **Story Act 2**.

- **WORD MATCHING** (Light blue text with a lock icon, Initially locked)  
  A drag-and-drop word matching game.  
  **Unlocks when you:** Earn **15 stars total in Emotion Matching**.

**Tip:** Locked modes show a lock icon. Hover over them to see the unlock conditions.

---

## Part 3: Story Mode Journey

### Story Act Selection

1. Press **STORY** from the mode select screen.
2. You'll see **Act 1**, **Act 2**, and **Act 3**.
3. **Act 1** is available immediately. Acts 2 and 3 unlock as you progress.
4. Select the act you want to play.

---

### Story Scene Gameplay

Once you enter an act, you see an interactive story scene with a character and dialogue.

**Story Scene Buttons:**

- **Skip Button** (Top-right corner, arrow icon)  
  Skip the current story beat (dialogue or animation). This will not skip the voiceover playback.

- **Pause Button** (Top-right, next to Skip)  
  Pauses the story. You can resume, adjust settings, or return to the menu from the pause menu.

- **Hearts Display** (Bottom-left corner)  
  Shows how many lives you have left. You start with 3 hearts per level. Losing all hearts means you fail the level.

- **Continue Automatically**  
  After dialogue finishes, press anywhere or wait for the next beat to continue.

---

### Emotion Selection Popup

At key moments in the story, a **popup appears** asking you to choose the correct emotion.

**What Happens:**

1. A scenario or image displays with a question like _"What emotion does the character feel?"_
2. You'll see buttons or icons for different emotions: **Joy**, **Sadness**, **Anger**, **Disgust**, **Fear**.
3. Tap the correct emotion.
4. If correct, a checkmark or success animation plays, and you earn a **star**.
5. If incorrect, you lose a **heart**. After all hearts are gone, you fail and can retry.

**Emotion Selection Tips:**

- Read the situation carefully.
- Each emotion has a distinct appearance (colors, expressions).
- You can make multiple attempts within a level as long as you have hearts.

---

### Lives (Hearts) System

**What are hearts?**

- You start each level with **3 hearts** (lives).
- Each wrong answer costs **1 heart**.
- If you run out of hearts, the level fails.

**After failing:**

- A popup appears offering to retry the level.
- You can try again or return to the main menu.

---

### Story Result Scene

After completing (or failing) a story beat or level, you see the **Result Screen**.

**Result Screen Shows:**

- **Compliment Message**
  - _"PERFECT!"_ (all hearts remaining)
  - _"GREAT JOB!"_ (some hearts used)
  - _"GOOD TRY!"_ (few hearts left)
  - _"YOU DID IT!"_ (completed successfully)

- **Level Number**  
  Current level or beat you just completed.

- **Coins Earned**  
  Bonus coins for clearing the level (varies by hearts remaining).

- **Next/Retry/Menu Buttons**
  - _Next_: Continue to the next story beat.
  - _Retry_: Try the level again.
  - _Menu_: Return to the mode select screen.

---

### Story Act 2 Completion

When you complete all of **Story Act 2**, a special popup appears:

**"EMOTION MATCHING UNLOCKED!"**  
You can now play the Emotion Matching game mode from the mode select screen.

---

## Part 4: Emotion Matching Mode

### Emotion Matching Character Selection

1. Press **EMOTION MATCHING** from the mode select screen.
2. You see a character selection screen with 5 emotion characters: **Joy**, **Sadness**, **Anger**, **Disgust**, **Fear**.
3. **Joy** is unlocked from the start.
4. Tap a character to enter their level select screen.

**Character Unlock Progression:**

- **Joy**: Always available.
- **Sadness, Anger, Disgust, Fear**: Unlock when you clear at least **3 levels** with the previous character.
- Locked characters are grayed out and show a lock icon.

**Next Button:**

- Press the _Next_ button to cycle through available characters.

---

### Emotion Level Select

After choosing a character, you see **5 levels** numbered 1–5.

**Level Unlock System:**

- **Level 1** is always unlocked for a character.
- **Level 2** unlocks after you clear **Level 1**.
- **Level 3** unlocks after you clear **Level 2**.
- And so on...

**Locked Levels:**

- Grayed out with a lock icon.
- You cannot play them until you clear the previous level.

**Star Display:**

- Each level shows stars earned in your best attempt.
- **1 star**: Cleared with 1 heart remaining.
- **2 stars**: Cleared with 2 hearts remaining.
- **3 stars**: Cleared with 3 hearts remaining (perfect).

**Selecting a Level:**

- Tap an unlocked level to start.

---

### Emotion Matching In-Game Scene

You enter a game screen with **5 emoticon buttons** (images of the emotion characters in different expressions).

**Game Layout:**

- **Situation Popup Button** (Center or top-right)  
  Tap this to see the situation/scenario you need to match.
- **Emoticons** (Arranged around the screen)  
  Tap the correct emoticon based on the situation shown.
- **Hearts Display** (Bottom-left)  
  Shows remaining lives. You start with 3 hearts.
- **Pause Button** (Top-right)  
  Pause the game to access settings or return to menu.

---

### The Situation Popup

When you tap the **Situation Popup Button**, an image appears showing a scenario.

**What to Do:**

1. Study the situation image carefully.
2. **Tap the matching emoticon** from the 5 buttons on the main screen.
3. If correct, you earn a **star** and the next situation appears.
4. If incorrect, you lose a **heart**. The same situation stays displayed.

**Important Mechanics:**

- **After the final correct emoticon**, the game **locks all input**.
- A voiceover plays explaining the emotion.
- You cannot click anything while waiting.
- After the voiceover finishes, the result scene appears automatically.

---

### Pause Menu (Emotion Matching)

Press the **Pause Button** during gameplay.

**Pause Menu Options:**

- **Resume**: Continue the game.
- **Hint**: Get a hint for the current situation (costs coins if available).
- **Settings**: Adjust audio/visuals.
- **Retry Level**: Restart the level from the beginning.
- **Main Menu**: Return to mode select.

**Note:** You **cannot** pause after selecting the final correct emoticon (input is locked).

---

### Emotion Matching Result Scene

After completing (or failing) a level, you see the **Result Screen**.

**Result Screen Details:**

**Top Section (Compliment):**

- _"PERFECT!"_ → Cleared with all 3 hearts.
- _"GREAT JOB!"_ → Cleared with 2 hearts.
- _"GOOD TRY!"_ → Cleared with 1 heart.
- _"TRY AGAIN!"_ → Failed (0 hearts).

**Middle Section (Stars & Coins):**

- **Stars Earned**: Shows 1–3 stars based on hearts remaining.
- **Coins**: Bonus coins for clearing (more stars = more coins).

**Hearts (❤️) Explanation:**

- **3 hearts remaining** = 3 stars = maximum reward.
- **2 hearts remaining** = 2 stars = good reward.
- **1 heart remaining** = 1 star = minimum reward.
- **0 hearts** = Failed, no stars or coins.

**Stars (⭐) Explanation:**

- Indicate your best performance on a level.
- **15 total stars across Emotion Matching levels** unlocks Word Matching.
- Stars accumulate and are displayed on the level select screen.

**Coins (🪙) Explanation:**

- Currency for in-game purchases (like hints).
- Earned based on level performance.
- Can be spent in pause menus to buy hints.

**Hints (💡) Explanation:**

- Help you identify the correct emotion.
- Cost 1–2 coins per hint.
- Can be purchased from the pause menu.
- Each level offers hints based on its difficulty.

**Bottom Buttons:**

- **Next**: Move to the next level (if unlocked).
- **Retry**: Replay the current level.
- **Level Select**: Return to the character's level select screen.
- **Main Menu**: Return to mode select.

---

### Character Unlocking System

**When you unlock a new character:**

1. Clear at least **3 levels** with a character (e.g., Joy).
2. A popup appears: _"[Character] Unlocked!"_ (e.g., _"Sadness Unlocked!"_)
3. You can now select that character from the character select screen.
4. That character's **Level 1** is unlocked.
5. You can repeat the process to unlock the next character.

**Character Unlock Order:**

- Joy → Sadness → Anger → Disgust → Fear

---

### Word Matching Unlocked

After earning **15 stars total in Emotion Matching**, a popup appears:

**"WORD MATCHING UNLOCKED!"**  
You can now play the Word Matching game mode from the mode select screen.

---

## Part 5: Word Matching Mode

### Word Matching Character Selection

1. Press **WORD MATCHING** from the mode select screen.
2. You see the same **5 emotion characters**.
3. **Joy** is unlocked from the start (same as Emotion Matching).
4. Tap a character to enter their level select screen.

**Character Unlock Progression:**

- **Joy**: Always available.
- **Sadness, Anger, Disgust, Fear**: Unlock by clearing at least **3 levels** with the previous character **in Word Matching**.
- **Note:** Character progress is **separate from Emotion Matching**. You must unlock them again in Word Matching.

---

### Word Matching Level Select

After choosing a character, you see **5 levels** numbered 1–5.

**Level Unlock System:**

- **Level 1** is always unlocked.
- **Levels 2–5** unlock as you clear previous levels (same as Emotion Matching).

**Star Display:**

- Shows stars earned in your best attempt.
- **1–3 stars** based on hearts remaining.

**Selecting a Level:**

- Tap an unlocked level to start.

---

### Word Matching In-Game Scene

You enter a game screen with **draggable word labels** and **target drop zones**.

**Game Layout:**

- **Situation Popup Button** (Center or top-right)  
  Tap to see the word matching scenario.
- **Word Labels** (Scattered on the left or bottom)  
  Tap and drag words to match them.
- **Target Zones** (On the right side)  
  Drop zones where words should be placed.
- **Hearts Display** (Bottom-left)  
  Shows remaining lives (start with 3).
- **Pause Button** (Top-right)  
  Pause to access menus.

---

### The Situation Popup (Word Matching)

When you tap the **Situation Popup Button**, a scenario image appears.

**What to Do:**

1. Study the situation image.
2. **Drag the correct word** from the word list to the matching target zone.
3. If correct, you earn a **star** and the next situation appears.
4. If incorrect, you lose a **heart**. The same situation stays displayed.

**Important Mechanics:**

- **After the final correct word**, the game **locks all input**.
- A voiceover plays explaining the scenario.
- You cannot interact while waiting.
- After the voiceover finishes, the result scene appears automatically.

---

### Pause Menu (Word Matching)

Press the **Pause Button** during gameplay.

**Pause Menu Options:**

- **Resume**: Continue the game.
- **Hint**: Get a hint for the current word (costs coins).
- **Settings**: Adjust audio/visuals.
- **Retry Level**: Restart the level from the beginning.
- **Main Menu**: Return to mode select.

**Note:** You **cannot** pause after the final correct word (input is locked).

---

### Word Matching Result Scene

After completing or failing a level, you see the **Result Screen** (identical to Emotion Matching).

**Result Screen Details:**

**Top Section (Compliment):**

- _"PERFECT!"_ → Cleared with all 3 hearts.
- _"GREAT JOB!"_ → Cleared with 2 hearts.
- _"GOOD TRY!"_ → Cleared with 1 heart.
- _"TRY AGAIN!"_ → Failed (0 hearts).

**Middle Section (Stars & Coins):**

- **Stars Earned**: 1–3 stars based on hearts remaining.
- **Coins**: Bonus currency earned.

**Hearts & Stars:** Same meanings as in Emotion Matching.

**Coins & Hints:** Same as Emotion Matching.

**Bottom Buttons:**

- **Next**: Move to the next level (if unlocked).
- **Retry**: Replay the current level.
- **Level Select**: Return to the character's level select screen.
- **Main Menu**: Return to mode select.

---

## Quick Reference: Game Flow

1. **Main Menu** → Press _Start Game_
2. **Mode Select** → Choose _Story_
3. **Act Select** → Choose _Act 1_
4. **Story Scenes** → Answer emotion questions, earn stars
5. **Earn Hearts & Coins** → Complete levels to unlock features
6. **Complete Story Act 2** → _Emotion Matching Unlocks_
7. **Character Select** → Choose _Joy_
8. **Level Select** → Play _Level 1_
9. **Emotion Matching** → Tap emotions, earn stars
10. **Unlock Characters** → Clear 3 levels per character
11. **Earn 15 Stars** → _Word Matching Unlocks_
12. **Word Matching** → Drag and drop words
13. **Keep Playing** → Unlock all characters and complete all levels

---

## Tips for Success

- **Read situations carefully** before selecting emotions or words.
- **Manage your hearts wisely**—more hearts remaining = more stars = more coins.
- **Use hints strategically** when you're unsure (they cost coins).
- **Pause** if you need a break or want to adjust settings.
- **Complete Acts in order** to unlock game modes.
- **Try to earn 3 stars** on each level for maximum rewards.
- **Unlock all characters** to experience the full game.
